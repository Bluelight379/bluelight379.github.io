DO NOT CLICK ON MALICIOUS LINKS

Just kidding! But seriously, phishing is a real threat. Here are some tips to avoid getting phished:

  1. Be suspicious of unsolicited emails or messages: Phishing attempts often come disguised as legitimate communications.
  2. Check the sender's email address: Look for misspellings or unusual domain names.
  3. Hover over links before clicking: Verify that the link leads to a legitimate website.
  4. Never enter personal information on unencrypted websites: Look for "https" in the URL and a padlock icon in the address bar.
  5. Keep your software up to date: Security updates often include protection against phishing attacks.
  6. Use a password manager: This helps generate and store strong, unique passwords.
  7. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA): This adds an extra layer of security to your accounts.
